Our School Life

Campus and Culture

The school life at Primary and Nursery School includes inter school exams with the private and governmental schools in Sinza Ward. We also compete in inter-school games and sports competitions which are normally done in february, march and april. Commonly known as UMITASHUMTA GAMES. Debates and Clubs make up the Primary and Secondary Schools Culture. The students can participate in English Debating Club, Computer Club, Social Studies Club, Mathematics Club, Science Club, Environmental Club and Kiswahili Club.

Peer Groups

Self-Organised learning

Peer Groups are independent student-orgnised learning groups that help learning partners to exchange knowledge and solve problems together. The groups are especially popular in preparation for national exams.

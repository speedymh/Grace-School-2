Who We Are

Experienced. Curious. International.

United by the believe in God, out Team of Teachers and Non-Teaching Staff has the aim of providing enlightment and education. We are thankful for the trust that parents give us and aware of the responsibility we have for the future life of our students. Besides our well-rehearsed team, Volunteers from worldwide enrich out lessons and create an athmosphere of cultural diversity.

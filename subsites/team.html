Who We Are

Experienced. Curious. International.

United by the believe in God, out Team of Teachers and Non-Teaching Staff has the aim of providing enlightment and education. We are thankful for the trust that parents give us and aware of the responsibility we have for the future life of our students. Besides our well-rehearsed team, Volunteers from worldwide enrich out lessons and create an athmosphere of cultural diversity.

Our Volunteers

Through our cooperation with United Planet Tanzania we are proud to be hosting a lot of volunteers coming from countries like Germany, Sweden, Italy, Switzerland, USA and others. Their task is to support us in English, Maths and PDS lessons. In addition to that we can understand a lot about each other through this type of cultural exchange which helps them to introduce different ways of thinking at our school.

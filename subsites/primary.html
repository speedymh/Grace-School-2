School Rules

  1. A pupil should arrive at school before 7.15am.
  2. A pupil should put on smart proper uniform every day.
  3. A pupil should communicate in English language. Kiswahili should be used only in Kiswahili lesson.
  4. A pupil should attend all lessons including Ethics/Values lessons.
  5. A pupil score below average shall force him/her to repeat the class.
  6. A pupil should not bring radio, telephone, ipod or other toys to school.
  7. Earrings, chains, rosary, decorations on hair, rastas, painting on nails, open shoes are not allowed.
  8. A pupil should not bring food and drinks at school.
  9. A pupil who is a noise maker, kiswahili speaker, fighter, abuser or a thief will be suspended from school.
  10. A pupil should have all exercise books and text books.
  11. A pupil is expected to do all class and homeworks.
  12. A pupil should attend assemblies and PE lesson.

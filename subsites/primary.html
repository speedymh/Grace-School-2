We Teach for the Future

ICT Lessons & Computer Classes

We prepare our students for the digitalized world. In ICT lessons the theoretical foundations get taught which are then turned into practice during the computer classes. Students learn to get around in popular Office-Programms as well as to work on presentations independently.

We Embrace Teambuilding and Self-Confidence

Weekly Sports Classes

School does not only have to consist of learning in the classroom, it can also mean understanding how to work togethter in a group and realizing the importance of a healthy lifestyle from the beginning on. In PDS classes topics get taught in a joyful way that is anyways still aware of the educational side of sports classes.

We Provide Education on Point

Saturday Classes for 4th and 7th Graders

We are around when things get important. In preparation for the 4th and 7th Grade Exams all students of these grades get additional lessons in the subjects of maths, science, english, kiswahili, history and geography on saturday. And what we do gets results: Our 4th and 7th Graders score continuously above average in the national exams.

School Rules

  1. A pupil should arrive at school before 7.15am.
  2. A pupil should put on smart proper uniform every day.
  3. A pupil should communicate in English language. Kiswahili should be used only in Kiswahili lesson.
  4. A pupil should attend all lessons including Ethics/Values lessons.
  5. A pupil score below average shall force him/her to repeat the class.
  6. A pupil should not bring radio, telephone, ipod or other toys to school.
  7. Earrings, chains, rosary, decorations on hair, rastas, painting on nails, open shoes are not allowed.
  8. A pupil should not bring food and drinks at school.
  9. A pupil who is a noise maker, kiswahili speaker, fighter, abuser or a thief will be suspended from school.
  10. A pupil should have all exercise books and text books.
  11. A pupil is expected to do all class and homeworks.
  12. A pupil should attend assemblies and PE lesson.

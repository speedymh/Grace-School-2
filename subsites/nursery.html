Our Motto

"Education is Life"

Nursery School is the most important step in the formal education of children. Here values get taught, that will guide every kid through his or her life. We are aware of that importance which is attended by a big responsability. To prepare our students for their educational path in Primary and Secondary School we foster their development particularly in the field of english and arithmetics. In these subjects we apply a variety of different learning methods that create an athmosphere in our school life that inspires and awakes the eager to learn for a whole life long.

We Teach a Strong Foundation

A big variety of Subjects

Our lessons include arithmetics, english, kiswahili, science and reading classes. This wide spread basis enables the children to explore a lot of different subjects which helps them to understand the world around them.

We Work with Children. And we are aware of that.

Sleeping Time over Noon

Every day after eating lunch the children of nursery school have time to sleep. Resting is an important piece in the physical and psychological development, it also helps to remember the just-learned things for a longer time which is why this part of the day has a very high priority for us.

School Rules

  1. A pupil should arrive at school before 7.15am.
  2. A pupil should put on smart proper uniform every day.
  3. A pupil should communicate in English language. Kiswahili should be used only in Kiswahili lesson.
  4. A pupil should attend all lessons including Ethics/Values lessons.
  5. A pupil should not bring radio, telephone, ipod or other toys to school.
  6. Earrings, chains, rosary, decorations on hair, rastas, painting on nails, open shoes are not allowed.
  7. A pupil should not bring food and drinks at school.
  8. A pupil who is a noise maker, kiswahili speaker, fighter, abuser or a thief will be suspended from school.
  9. A pupil should have all exercise books and text books.
  10. A pupil is expected to do all class and homeworks.
  11. A pupil should attend assemblies and PE lesson.
  12. A pupil visiting baby class (2-3 years old) leaves school after lunch before the sleeping break.
